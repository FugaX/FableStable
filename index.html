Fable Stable

FableStable is a web-based application in which you can manage your writing projects, collaborate with people on them, and recruit people for jobs.

Get Started

About

April 7, 2017

FableStable was created in late 2017 and later developed in 2018 using Bootstrap.

Why FableStable?

April 7, 2017

FableStable, unlike many colllaborative writing applications give users a new insight on collaboration and allow users to recruit writers from here.

Features

Project Boards

FableStable simplifies the onus of collaborative projects by adding a board that allows you to add tasks and assign them to people that are collaborating along with you.

Job Recruitment

FableStable allows you to link up with publishers for your stories, recruit people based on their profiles for jobs, and find people to collaborate with on future endeavors.

Novel Deployment

FableStable helps you manage the novel deployment workflow by helping you find places where you can deploy your stories!

Shelving

FableStable allows you to store and save all of your files in containers called "shelves" in which you can also invite users to work on and deploy them.
